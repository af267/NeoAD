RL-X

MADE FROM 100% RECYCLED MATERIALS

The NeoAD RL-X is a reusable model rocket made out of 100% recycled materials including paper towel rolls, cardboard boxes, and plastic trash bags. The vehicle is powered by a single 18mm black powder solid rocket motor. With an Estes C6-5 motor, the rocket has an apogee of around 800 ft.

SPECIFICATIONS

HEIGHT: 12-13 in.

DIAMETER: 1.5 in.

MASS: 2.5 oz.

ENGINE: 18 mm. A/B/C

APOGEE: ~800 ft.

RL-Y

3D PRINTED CORE

The NeoAD RL-Y is a model rocket with a 3D printed core stage made with a school printer. It has a 2.56 in. diameter payload bay capable of carrying payloads up to 2 oz. With an Estes F15 engine fitted in, the vehicle is capable of reaching altitudes of around 540 ft. The first vehicle, Y1001, is slated to fly NET August 2023.

SPECIFICATIONS

HEIGHT: 30 in.

DIAMETER: 2.56 in.

PAYLOAD: 2 oz.

ENGINE: 29 mm. D/E/F

APOGEE: ~540 ft.
